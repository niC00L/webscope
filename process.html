Process makes projects successful

The main key is to understand our client

Open communication

Without understanding our clients it is irrelevant how technically qualified is our staff. We focus on open communication and transparency. We are not afraid to define the problem and we expect our clients to be forward with us as well. Therefore we can always find the best solution even if it means to end the cooperation.

Responsibility in deadlines

Keeping up with deadlines shows mutual respect. If we need more time for a task we inform our clients in advance and we expect the same form them. Time spent waiting on documents, files or information is time lost.

Client over product

We put our clients in a first place. We try to listen to better understand the business value of given project. It doesn't matter how technically advanced is the product, it needs to work for a client.

Value for money

We bring value to the client, so he can generate more profit. We set our price fairly with regard to our capabilities. That is why our clients pay their invoices when the job is done to show that they appreciate our work. We offer lower rates and discounts only to open source and non-profit projects that we believe in or start-ups.
